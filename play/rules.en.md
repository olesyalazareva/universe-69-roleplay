# UNIVERSE 69: Game Rules v1.0

## 📦 What You Need

- One standard UNO deck (108 cards) or any compatible deck
- 6 "Chance" tokens per player (poker chips, coins, or any markers)
- 2-9 players
- One dealer (can also be a player)

---

## 🎭 Before the Game: Choose an Avatar

Each player selects an avatar from `avatars.md`:
- **Light** — easier, softer archetypes (for beginners or light sessions)
- **Hard** — deeper, shadow archetypes (for experienced players)

You can also create your own avatar following the same structure.

---

## 🃏 Card Values & Difficulty

| Range | Difficulty | Description |
|-------|------------|-------------|
| 2-5 | Low (warm-up) | Compliments, cocktails, light actions |
| 6-9 | Medium (unfolding) | Fantasies, hugs, submission |
| 10-Ace | High (depth) | Sensations, experiments, undressing, domination |
| Joker | Maximum (chaos) | Complete rule breaking |

**Full list of tasks — in `cards.md`**

---

## 🎲 Setup

1. **Shuffle** the entire deck
2. **Deal 6 cards** face down to each player
3. **Give 6 Chance tokens** to each player
4. Place the **remaining deck** face down — this is the Draw pile
5. Turn over **1 card** from the Draw pile — this is the Discard pile starter
6. **Dealer** goes first, then clockwise

---

## 🔄 Game Loop (One Turn)
Declare which card you're playing

Announce the task from the card

Role-play the task (speak truth through your character)

Group votes: BELIEVE or DON'T BELIEVE

If BELIEVE → keep your Chance token, next player

If DON'T BELIEVE → lose 1 Chance token

Discard the played card

Draw until you have exactly 6 cards

## 🃏 Card Playing Rules (UNO Mechanics)

### Basic rule
You can only play a card if it matches:
- The **suit** (color) of the top card, OR
- The **number** of the top card

### Special cards

| Card | Effect |
|------|--------|
| **Skip** | Next player skips their turn |
| **Reverse** | Direction of play reverses |
| **Draw Two (+2)** | Next player draws 2 cards and skips turn |
| **Wild** | Change suit to any color (can only be played on first move of the game) |
| **Wild Draw Four (+4)** | Change suit + next player draws 4 cards |

### If you cannot play
Draw 1 card from the Draw pile:
- If it matches → play it immediately
- If not → keep it, turn passes

---

## 🎭 Role-Play Rules

### What you MUST do:
- **Speak in first person** as your avatar
- **Follow the card's task** literally
- **Be honest** — reveal real reactions through your character

### What you CANNOT do:
- **Refuse** a task (unless it's a personal hard boundary — announce before game)
- **Lie** about your feelings/reactions
- **Break character** during your turn

### Boundaries (Hard Limits)
Before the game, each player announces 1-3 hard limits. These tasks are excluded from that player's gameplay.

---

## ✅ Verification: "BELIEVE or DON'T BELIEVE"

After each role-play, ALL players (including dealer) vote:

| Vote | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **BELIEVE** | The role-play was truthful, in character, and followed the task |
| **DON'T BELIEVE** | The player broke character, lied, or avoided the task |

### Voting rules:
- Vote simultaneously (raise hand or say "B" / "DB")
- Dealer's vote counts as 1
- Majority decides

### Consequence of "DON'T BELIEVE":
The player loses **1 Chance token**.

---

## 💀 Losing the Game

A player is eliminated when they lose **all 6 Chance tokens**.

**Last player standing wins.**

---

## 🃏 Joker (Chaos Mode)

When a Joker is played:
1. The player **invents a new rule** (any rule)
2. The new rule lasts for **one full circle** (until it returns to this player)
3. The player also **chooses the next task** (any from `cards.md`)

## 📝 Example Turn

**Top card:** Red 5

**Player plays:** Red 7

**Card task (7 of Red):** *"Describe your first sexual fantasy without embarrassment"*

**Player role-plays (as "Constrained Accountant"):** *"I look at you, my hands shaking, and whisper: 'I fantasize about someone counting my breaths. Exactly 37 breaths. No more, no less. Then I can relax.'"*

**Group vote:** "BELIEVE" (majority)

**Result:** Player keeps Chance token, turn ends.
